Introduction to the Different Types of Doctors

Doctors and their specialties are based on the type of care they provide, the types of people they treat, and the area of the body they focus on. Types of doctors can include:

  • Medical specialists
  • Surgical specialists

Medical Specialists

There are three medical specialties: internal medicine, family medicine, and pediatrics.

Internal Medicine

Internal medicine doctors specialize in adults and focus on preventive care and the treatment of disease.

Family Medicine

Family care doctors specialize in adults, children, and in some cases, pregnant females. They provide primary care, preventive medicine, and emergency medical care.

Pediatrics

Pediatric doctors focus on caring for babies and children.
